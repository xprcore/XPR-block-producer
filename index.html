About XPRCORE

XPRCORE is a reliable and dedicated block producer on the XPR Network, committed to supporting the growth, security, and decentralization of the system. We actively contribute to the development and promotion of the XPR Network blockchain, ensuring stable, secure, and uninterrupted node operations. We collaborate with the community, support education and knowledge expansion about XPR technology, and encourage cooperation with other developers and partners within the network.
